# Trainer Standard Operating Procedure

## Document purpose

This document provides operational guidance for trainers delivering enterprise learning programs. It covers session preparation, delivery, communication, attendance, assessments, project mentoring, reporting, evidence collection, and escalation.

---

## 1. Trainer attire and presentation

- Wear formal attire during in-person and virtual sessions.
- Keep the camera switched on during virtual sessions.

---

## 2. Language for training delivery

- Use English as the primary language during training.
- Use a regional language when learners have difficulty understanding a concept.

---

## 3. Batch formation and learner verification

### First session

- Verify the registered learner list received from the program management team.

### Second day of training

- Create and finalise the batch in the Learning Management System using the list of regular learners.

### Discrepancies

- Inform the team lead and program manager if the learner list contains missing, incorrect, or duplicate records.

---

## 4. Session reminders and learner communication

- Use the official college WhatsApp group for training-related communication.
- Send a session reminder 15 minutes before the session.
- Include the session details and meeting link in the reminder.
- Respond to learner questions during the designated daily support period, subject to trainer availability.

---

## 5. Attendance management

- Mark learner attendance in the Learning Management System every day.
- Attendance should not be left pending after the session.
- Escalate recurring learner attendance problems to the team lead and program manager.

---

## 6. Content delivery

- Follow the approved content planner.
- Deliver topics according to the planned schedule.
- Use only the approved program content available in the central repository.
- Do not use unapproved or outdated locally stored content.

---

## 7. Virtual session recordings

- Record all virtual training sessions.
- Upload recordings to the approved YouTube playlist or designated repository.
- Share the recording with learners 30 to 45 minutes after the session.

---

## 8. Assessments

- Assign assessments through the Learning Management System according to the content planner.
- Ensure regular learners complete assessments within the allocated time.
- Follow up with learners who have pending assessments.
- Escalate repeated non-completion where intervention is required.

---

## 9. Capstone projects and case studies

- Mentor learners on projects and case studies included in the content planner.
- Support learners in developing quality project submissions.
- Ensure projects are submitted within the agreed timeline.

---

## 10. Capstone project evaluation

- Require learners to submit projects using the approved project template.
- Conduct two formal project evaluations:
  - Mid-term evaluation
  - Final-term evaluation
- Record project scores in the Learning Management System.

---

## 11. Learner feedback

- Encourage learners to submit feedback after each session.
- Capture feedback through the Learning Management System.
- Review constructive feedback and use it to improve delivery.

---

## 12. Completion report

- Prepare the batch completion report after the training ends.
- Upload the completion report to the designated repository.
- Submit it within one week of batch completion.

---

## 13. Session photographs and evidence

### In-person sessions

- Capture clear, high-quality session photographs.

### Virtual sessions

- Capture relevant screenshots.

### Storage

- Store all photographs and screenshots in the designated repository for the relevant institution or college.

---

## 14. Handling operational challenges

Immediately inform the team lead and program manager when there are issues related to:

- College schedules
- Learner attendance
- Batch formation
- Repeated technical issues
- Delayed assessments
- Project submission risks

---

## Frequently asked operational questions

### When should a trainer send the session reminder?

Send it 15 minutes before the session with the meeting link and session details.

### When should a virtual-session recording be shared?

Share it 30 to 45 minutes after the session.

### How often should attendance be marked?

Attendance should be marked daily in the Learning Management System.

### When is the completion report due?

The completion report should be uploaded within one week of batch completion.

### How many project evaluations are required?

Two evaluations are required: one at mid-term and one at final term.

### What should a trainer do when a college schedule or attendance issue occurs?

Inform the team lead and program manager immediately.
