# Trainer Standard Operating Procedure

## Purpose

This document provides operational guidance for trainers delivering enterprise learning programs.

---

## Before Session

- Review trainer guide
- Verify learner list
- Test all labs
- Confirm internet connectivity
- Check presentation deck

---

## During Session

- Deliver approved content
- Encourage learner participation
- Track attendance
- Resolve learner questions

---

## After Session

- Upload recording
- Share learning material
- Assign assessments
- Submit attendance
- Escalate technical issues
