# Business Problem

Large learning programs often support multiple learner groups, trainers, institutions, and internal stakeholders.

Common operational challenges include:

- Repeated trainer and learner questions
- Difficulty locating current learning content
- Manual certification tracking
- Inconsistent reporting processes
- Limited visibility into learner risks
- Time-consuming preparation of learning plans
- Fragmented communication across teams
- Delays in escalating attendance or completion issues

These problems reduce the time available for high-value learning design, coaching, stakeholder engagement, and program improvement.

## Opportunity

An AI-powered learning operations assistant can provide immediate, role-specific support while helping learning teams standardise processes and reduce repetitive work.

## Target Users

- Learners
- Trainers
- Program Managers
- Learning Administrators
- Learning and Development Teams
- AI Enablement Teams
